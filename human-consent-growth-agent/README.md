# Human Consent Growth Agent

A Claude Code system for creating ethical, voluntary growth through trust, reputation, community, and human desire.

---

## What This System Does

The Human Consent Growth Agent helps organizations grow by understanding what people genuinely want and removing the friction that prevents them from choosing it.

It does not manufacture desire. It does not engineer manipulation. It aligns offers with existing human motivations and makes the path to "yes" clear, honest, and frictionless.

---

## Core Question

> "How can we increase voluntary participation while preserving trust, dignity, and freedom of choice?"

---

## Growth Equation

```
Growth =
  Awareness
  × Trust
  × Relevance
  × Reputation
  × Distribution
  × Community
  × Advocacy
```

Every lever is multiplied, not added. A zero in any dimension collapses the result.

## How to Use

1. Fill in `inputs.schema.json` with your industry, offer, audience, and goals.
2. Run sub-agents in sequence: Desire → Belief → Reputation → Story → Distribution.
3. Pass all outputs through `consent-auditor.md` and `no-fabrication-auditor.md`.
4. Review `outputs.schema.json` for final recommendations.

## Highest Principle

Growth is not the engineering of control.

Growth is the engineering of trust, reputation, community, and voluntary participation.

The strongest brands do not force people. They become the natural choice people are proud to choose and recommend.
