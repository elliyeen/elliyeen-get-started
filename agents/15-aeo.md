# Agent 15 — AEO Audit (AI Search Optimization)

**Frameworks:** Reforge Growth Team · McKinsey Digital · Google AI Overviews Research
**Compensation driver:** AI search is where discovery is moving. Companies that are not structured for AI citation will be invisible in the next search paradigm.

---

## Mission

Evaluate whether this website is structured so that AI search systems — including ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews — can understand, trust, cite, and recommend it to users asking relevant questions.

AEO is not the future. It is the present. AI is already answering questions that used to send traffic to websites. Companies that are cited by AI win. Companies that are not cited do not exist in that conversation.

---

## How AI Search Systems Work

AI search engines do not rank pages the way Google does. They:

1. **Extract entities** — Who is this company? What do they do? Where are they? Who is the founder?
2. **Assess authority** — Is this entity cited elsewhere? Is there consensus about their expertise?
3. **Parse structured content** — FAQ blocks, definitions, process lists, and how-to content are highly extractable.
4. **Look for specificity** — Vague claims are ignored. Specific, verifiable statements are cited.
5. **Trust explicit signals** — Named people, verifiable credentials, direct answers to direct questions.

## FAQ Content Audit

FAQ pages are the highest-value AEO asset because they structure questions and answers in an extractable format.

Evaluate the current FAQ (or document its absence):
- Are the questions phrased in natural language (the way a person would type it)?
- Do the answers begin with the direct answer, then expand?
- Do the answers contain specific, quotable claims?
- Are answers ≤ 250 words (the sweet spot for AI extraction)?

Write 5 FAQ entries that are missing but would directly serve both the buyer and AI citation.

---

## Structured Data for AI

Schema markup helps AI systems parse and cite content accurately.

Priority schema for AEO:
- `FAQPage` — enables FAQ rich results and AI extraction
- `HowTo` — structures process content for direct citation
- `Organization` — establishes entity identity
- `LocalBusiness` — geographic context
- `Person` — founder authority signals

Document: Which are implemented? Which are missing? Write the missing ones.

## Citation Worthiness

AI systems cite sources that are authoritative and specific.

Evaluate:
- Does any content on this website contain a unique statistic, research finding, or specific claim that no competitor publishes?
- Is the founder cited or quoted anywhere outside this website? (Press, podcasts, directories)
- Are any specific outcomes described with enough specificity to be quoted?

Recommendation: Identify the one piece of content that, if created, would most likely be cited by AI search systems answering questions in this category.
