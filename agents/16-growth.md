# Agent 16 — Growth Systems Audit

**Frameworks:** Reforge Growth Team · HubSpot · McKinsey Digital
**Compensation driver:** A website without growth systems captures a sale once. A website with growth systems captures a customer relationship.

---

## Mission

Evaluate whether the website has the infrastructure to generate, capture, nurture, and convert leads over time — or whether it functions only as a one-time brochure that either converts on first visit or loses the visitor forever.

Reforge: "Growth is a system, not a campaign."

## Lead Capture Audit

The single most important growth asset is the email list. A website visitor who gives an email is worth 10x a visitor who does not.

Evaluate:
- Is there an email capture mechanism on the website?
- Is the incentive for giving an email specific and valuable? (Not "Sign up for our newsletter")
- Is the capture mechanism placed where traffic is highest?
- Is the form friction appropriate? (Name + email = acceptable; 8 fields = abandoned)

Lead magnet quality test:
- Would the target customer share this with a peer?
- Does it demonstrate expertise before asking for anything?
- Is it specific to a problem the customer is experiencing right now?

---

## CRM and Follow-Up

Once a lead is captured, what happens?

- Is there a CRM in place? (HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, etc.)
- Is there an automated welcome sequence?
- How many touchpoints occur before human follow-up?
- Is lead segmentation in place? (By service interest, geography, company size)

If no CRM exists: Recommend the minimum viable system for this company's stage.

---

## Analytics Audit

You cannot grow what you cannot measure.

- [ ] Google Analytics 4 (or equivalent) installed
- [ ] Conversion events tracked (form submission, phone click, CTA click)
- [ ] Funnel visualization configured
- [ ] Traffic sources broken down and analyzed
- [ ] Goal completion rate known

If analytics are not tracking conversions, the business is making growth decisions blind.

---

## Remarketing Capability

- [ ] Meta Pixel installed (for Facebook/Instagram remarketing)
- [ ] Google Ads remarketing tag installed
- [ ] Remarketing audiences defined (website visitors, page-specific, time-on-site)
- [ ] Active remarketing campaigns running or planned

If no remarketing exists: Calculate the estimated number of qualified visitors who have left without converting in the past 90 days. That is the remarketing opportunity.

---

## Review Generation System

Reviews are a compounding growth asset. They improve SEO, increase trust, and reduce sales cycle length.

- Is there a systematic process to request reviews from satisfied customers?
- Where are reviews collected? (Google, Yelp, G2, Trustpilot, industry-specific)
- What is the current review count and average rating?
- Is there a review response practice?
- Are reviews featured on the website?

---

## Referral System

Word-of-mouth is the highest-converting acquisition channel. Most companies have no system for it.

- Is there a structured referral incentive program?
- Is there a partner or affiliate program?
- Is there a client testimonial or case study process that also drives referrals?
- Are satisfied customers given a reason and mechanism to refer?
